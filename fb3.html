How It Works

What does Revords do?

Our powerful marketing program excels at driving a higher influx of customers into your store.

Establish automated systems for rewards and promotions

We assist you in developing a digital rewards program that offers customers points and gifts based on their purchases. Furthermore, we create a series of automated campaigns designed to welcome new customers, commemorate their birthdays, and re-engage those who haven't visited your business recently.

Your customers can effortlessly sign up and check in by using their phone number

Customers have the option to sign up either in-store or through our mobile app, ensuring that you gather accurate customer information gradually, all while maintaining a smooth and efficient checkout process.

Our campaigns are automatically sent and deliver 10X greater effectiveness.

With our AutoPilot feature, your customers receive special offers through app notifications, text messages, or emails automatically, resulting in a remarkable 10x increase in effectiveness compared to other marketing tools.

Send single-use promotions that can boost customer visits by 70%

Utilize our promotions tool to generate tailored promotions aimed at all customers, new customers, or specific groups of your choosing. One-time promotions serve as an excellent method to showcase special events or sales.

Easily redeem promotions from your POS or tablet device with the assistance of your staff

Our software facilitates personalized customer interactions by enabling your staff to not only redeem customers' rewards effortlessly but also learn their names and preferences.

Your customers will adore the personalized promotions they receive, and the impressive results will reflect their satisfaction.

Each time a customer signs up, checks in, or redeems a reward, their activity is tracked on your Revords Dashboard. Witness the seamless collaboration between AutoPilot and Promotions as they work together to re-engage your customers more swiftly.
