Customer Aquisition

Encounter your future ideal client

Acquiring new customers is a crucial aspect of your business's expansion, and it doesn't have to be a source of stress. That's why Revords has developed solutions that harness the satisfaction of your customers and the potential of your network to provide effortless, cost-effective, and remarkably successful customer acquisition strategies.

Revord's approach to acquiring customers reimagines the process.

Revords Acquisition simplifies the process for local businesses to connect with our vast network of 50 million loyal customers who actively support local establishments. By effectively capturing the information of interested customers, converting them into in-store visitors, and implementing retargeting strategies, we ensure their continued patronage. Our goal is to streamline customer acquisition and engagement, providing local businesses with an effortless and automated solution that yields exceptional results. Key advantages of Revords Acquisition include: ⚡Expand your customer database by up to 2,000 new customers annually ⚡Attract high-quality shoppers who visit your business five times within their first year ⚡Establish and nurture enduring customer relationships



Igniting exploration...



In-store

Your business will be showcased on the Customer Touchscreen located at nearby Revords-affiliated establishments that are not direct competitors. Our platform displays the enticing rewards you have established for customer acquisition. When interested customers save these rewards, they are automatically added to your database, enabling you to send them additional offers and incentives to encourage their visits to your business.

In-App and Website

When customers access the Revords app or browse our website, they will encounter a map showcasing the proximity of various Revords-affiliated businesses, providing them with an additional opportunity to discover your establishment. Additionally, we actively promote nearby local businesses through SMS (text messaging) campaigns, ensuring heightened visibility and awareness among potential customers.

Maintaining a prominent presence in customers' minds...

Once a customer saves your reward, whether it's through the Customer Touchscreen or our mobile app, we will seamlessly and automatically engage them over time, ensuring that your business remains at the forefront of their thoughts. Leveraging their behavior as a guide, we will employ retargeting techniques to effectively engage and nurture your potential customers. The following approaches will be utilized to achieve this:

Through intelligent retargeting techniques...

1️⃣ Within one hour of their visit to a Revords business, the customer will receive an SMS message that highlights the reward they saved from your business.

2️⃣ A reminder will be sent (via SMS, push notification, or email) two days prior to the expiration of the saved reward, ensuring customers are prompted to take advantage of it.

3️⃣ For customers who haven't visited your business within 31 days of saving the reward, an automatic distribution of a new redeemable reward will be initiated to encourage their return.

The power of personal recommendations...

Recommendations from family and friends might be the most effective marketing there is, yielding twice the sales of paid advertising. The Revords Word of Mouth feature adds sharing to the power of customer loyalty—and acquires a whole new group of customers.

Features


Automated and shareable promotions on autopilot...

Each offer you send, whether it is triggered automatically through AutoPilot or sent as a one-time promotion using the Promotions tool, can now be easily shared by your devoted customers.

Shareable through text

Upon receiving a deal via text, your customers will have the option to share it using a pre-populated message that includes a link to your offer. This makes it effortless for them to spread the word and promote your deal to others.

Tap to accept and share

Every message sent through AutoPilot and Promotions features a trackable link and a compelling call to action: "Tap to accept and share with friends." By tapping on the link, customers are directed to a mobile landing page where they can conveniently share the deal with their network, expanding its reach through their personal connections.

Controls on Dashboard

You have the flexibility to choose which deals you want to make shareable. By default, all campaigns and promotions, except for Birthday offers, will be enabled. However, you have the option to modify this setting anytime through your AutoPilot configuration or when setting up a one-time deal using the Promotions tool. This gives you full control over the shareability of your deals according to your preferences.
