About Us

The visionary minds behind our company

Victor and Matt embarked on their professional journey as esteemed consultants at McKinsey, where they gained firsthand experience of the impact customer loyalty programs can have. Recognizing the significant investments made by large corporations in building such programs, they envisioned a future where the power of Fortune 500 loyalty initiatives could be accessible to local businesses. From humble beginnings as two individuals in a garage, Revords has grown to serve countless businesses nationwide. In October 2021, Revords achieved a major milestone by joining forces with SumUp, a renowned global payments leader.

Our Guiding Principles

Our guiding principles serve as a constant reminder that our approach to work is equally significant to the tasks we undertake.

Collective Modesty

Each one of us brings our distinct talents to the table, recognizing the importance of collaboration and depending on others for our achievements. Embracing shared humility, we foster a culture where success is celebrated collectively, and failures become valuable learning experiences for the entire team.

Indomitable Determination.

Once they developed the initial product, Vic and Matt tirelessly hit the streets, working around the clock to secure the company's first 100 sales. At times, we must dig deep, embodying a warrior spirit, and outperform the competition to elevate the company to new heights.

Genuine Connections

At our workplace, we embrace authenticity by being true to ourselves. This entails not only showcasing our strengths but also being open about our vulnerabilities. By fostering genuine and transparent connections, we cultivate a foundation of trust and encouragement that empowers us to take bold risks.

Daily Delight.

By embodying our values on a daily basis, we discover a profound sense of joy each day. When Victor speaks of joy, he refers to a nuanced understanding: it arises from a profound sense of purpose and satisfaction, and it can be experienced amidst challenges as well as achievements.
