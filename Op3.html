Marketing Automation

Introducing AutoPilot

AutoPilot, the core tool of the Fivestars program, takes center stage by automatically delivering targeted offers to your customers. Designed specifically with the needs of local businesses and their busy owners in mind, AutoPilot provides perfectly-timed prompts that entice customers to return and increase their spending.

Deliver the ideal message precisely when it matters the most.

AutoPilot operates on a set of triggers that are tied to significant milestones in a customer's journey, such as their initial visit, birthday, or potential risk of not returning. The best part is that once AutoPilot is activated, it runs fully on autopilot, meticulously monitoring your customers and their rewards, eliminating the need for manual tracking on your part.

Sign-up

Offering a reward upon sign-up provides customers with a compelling incentive, making their initial experience with your business even more enticing. It's an excellent way to extend a warm welcome and establish a positive connection from the very beginning.

First Visit

Research shows that more than 60% of new customers fail to return after their initial visit. However, by providing an additional reward, you can substantially reduce this percentage and increase customer retention.

At-Risk

Studies indicate that a customer's probability of returning diminishes by 50-75% every 30 days. At Fivestars, we assist you in proactively engaging with at-risk customers, ensuring that you connect with them before it's too late.

Lost

Recover customers who have been inactive for a period ranging from five months to a year.

Growth

Approximately 80% of your revenue is generated by repeat customers. By sending them targeted promotions, you can boost their visit rates by 25% or even higher. Moreover, when these customers do visit, they tend to spend more, resulting in increased revenue for your business.

Network

Reconnect with customers who initially discovered your business through the Fivestars network by employing tailored messaging that entices them to return to your store. By delivering targeted communication, you can effectively bring these customers back and reinforce their engagement with your business.

Lapsed

Utilizing the lapsed campaign can significantly enhance the chances of a customer returning to your business, with the potential to increase their likelihood by up to four times. This targeted campaign focuses on re-engaging customers who have lapsed, resulting in higher customer retention rates and increased business growth.

Birthday

Offer your customers an extra reason to celebrate by choosing your business as the destination for their celebration.
